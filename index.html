Understanding Wall Street Memes

Wall Street Memes is a decentralized protocol built on the Ethereum blockchain that enables the tokenization of future yield from DeFi protocols. It allows users to trade and invest in future yield streams, transforming them into fungible tokens known as Pendle Tokens (PTs). By tokenizing yield, Wall Street Memes provides users with a new level of flexibility, liquidity, and risk management in their DeFi investments.
